Pottas Farm World

Pottas Farms is an eco friendly farm at Adimali in Kerala, India adopting value addition methods for spices and plantation crops. Presently we add value to vanilla ,clove, cinnamon, cardamom,ginger,garlic,star anise,P.jeera,Nutmeg and mixed spices by extraction and make colorants from turmeric, paprika and spinach
